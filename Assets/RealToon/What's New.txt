What's New to RealToon V5.2.2 Update (March 2019)
=====================================
[New]

- Directional Light Intensity [New Option/Feature]
* You can now adjust "Directional Light Intensity" on a material, 
  just like "Point & Spot Light Intensity".

- No Light and Shadow On Backface [New Option/Feature]
* For Double Sided "On".

- Far Distance Max Width [New Option/Feature]
* Maximum outline width when far.
* An option for "Outline Width Affected By View Distance".

[Other New]
- Video Tutorials [Button]
* To access "Video Tutorials" right away.

- Added support "GLES 2.0".

[Other Notes]
* If you encounter some problems or need help just contact by email.
